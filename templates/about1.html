What inspired HandSignify?

Personal experiences with communication barriers within the deaf community inspired us to create a tool that would bridge the gap between sign language and spoken language.

How does HandSignify work?

HandSignify translates sign language gestures into spoken words and vice versa, making conversations more seamless for users.

Is HandSignify accessible everywhere?

HandSignify is designed to be accessible on a variety of devices, ensuring equal access to communication for everyone.

What's next for HandSignify?

We're constantly improving and expanding our features, aiming to include new languages and dialects to reach a wider audience.
